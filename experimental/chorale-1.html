Explanation for people who cannot read Japanese:
In an ASMR-like whisper, the artist reads out text printed on the various packaging containers shown in this photo. There are alcohol and green tea packs, earphones, etc. What stands out in this photo is a pack of inexpensive sake from a brand called “Oni-koroshi 鬼ころし,” which simply translates to “Devil Killer.” The title of this work comes from this.
